We are a family company!

The founder Gandalf The White, a well-known worker from Calgary-AB, prospected many decades ago the opportunity to explore and expand a unique business. Along with his son Geralt and his daughter Ciri, Gandalf started pretty simple: offering to neighbors snow removal services. In the very first beginning, both Gandalf and Geralt worked with manual equipment being only snow shovel and their grit to get the job done, while Ciri took over the accounting and a straightforward merchandise - knocking from door to door.

Their quality service returned an incredible demand and they found necessary to hire another important actual member - Aragorn, the Gandalf's nephew. Few months after, they invested in their first automated machine and professional chemicals.

In 2000, a brand new company was born: Smart Frost Inc.!

Since then, the company has been expanding its fleet with modern tractors and equipment, has started offering and fostering training for employees, and with partners, reached nearby cities (Edmonton and Red Deer). Smart Frost established a new standard in the industry and is considered the main snow removal services provider of Calgary.

"Our task is an art for people who like it smart!" Gandalf - Smart Frost founder.
