Smart Frost is a family company!

The founder Gandalf the White, a well-known worker from Calgary, prospected the potential market to be explored and developed through his services and sketched a new business with his son Geralt and his daughter Ciri.

Gandalf and Geralt started removing snow manually with snow shovels and plows in their neighborhoods, while Ciri took over the accounts.

Due to a great success and a lot of demand, they invested in their first automated machine, professional chemicals, and Gandalf recruited his nephew, Aragorn.

In 2000, a brand new company was born - Smart Frost Inc.!

Since then, Smart Frost expanded the business working with partners, expanded its fleet including modern tractors for major services, fostering complete trainings for its employees, and nowadays it is considered the main snow removal services provider of Calgary and nearby cities (Edmonton and Red Deer).

"Our business is an art for people who like it smart!" - Gandalf, Smart Frost founder.
